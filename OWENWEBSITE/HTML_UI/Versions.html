I am completely using 100% HTML and CSS to design this Digital CV. While there are many smart and fast ways to create a webpage and design, I chose the traditional approach. The reason is that I want to show you the effort and dedication I put into building something I love to learn and create. I understand that PDF resumes are also considered old-fashioned, so I created my own Digital Webpage to give you a glimpse of the future of resumes, making it easier for you to view and understand my skills and experiences. And of course! I will update a new design of UI/UX and to approach the other smart Tools and Softwares.

I want to showcase my skills through my Digital CV. I will continuously update it because I consider myself the product. I aim to demonstrate and prove my abilities to others, highlighting my knowledge and skills.

Creating this Digital CV not only allows me to present my knowledge and experience but also provides a better and easier way for future hiring managers or anyone responsible for hiring to understand my qualifications. This Digital CV is not just for fun; it represents me as the product, showcasing my knowledge, thinking, building, and learning abilities.
